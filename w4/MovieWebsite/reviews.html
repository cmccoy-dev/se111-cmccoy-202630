All the reviews about Revenge of the Sith!

Cayden McCoy April 14th, 2026

Throughout my life I have many favorite movies with my top five constantly changing. However Revenge of the Sith has always been my favorite. The opening scene places us right into the action with a battle taking place right above the planet of Courisant. This scene is done perfectly from a cinemotagraphy perspective and helps to hook the viewer into staying for what is to come. And then skip past all the action scenes in the middle and towards the end you are met with some of the most emotionally deep storytelling I have ever seen. From Obi-Wan learning that his brother has turned to the dark side, the killing of all the jedi, Anakin's failure to deal with inner conflict, to Anakin's heartbeat stopping as soon as the iconic mask of Darth Vader is put on. It's just truely incredible what Lucasfilm was able to do. And I haven't even been able to talk about the final action scene with Obi-Wan and Anakin yet. I could go on and on about this movie, but to wrap it up all I have to say is that it is a must watch, and it has offically been able to make the history books for some of the greatest movies of all time.
